Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need special hardware or cameras?

No. A regular phone or camera photo of the PCB works — the model is built to detect defects from standard images, not specialized AOI hardware.

How accurate is the detection?

We're transparent about accuracy and continuously improving the model. Current performance data is shared directly with paying customers as part of onboarding.

What happens after my 5 free scans?

You can upgrade to a paid plan anytime — Starter, Professional, or Enterprise — depending on your monthly scan volume.

Can you train the model on our specific PCB types?

Yes — that's the Custom Training tier. We retrain the detection model on your actual PCB designs and defect types, typically within 2 weeks.

Is there an API?

Yes, available on the Professional plan and above, or as a standalone API Integration package if you need it connected to existing systems.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes, subscriptions are month-to-month with no long-term contract required.
